# Design Approval

## Overview

The design of the AI-Based Medical Report Analyzer has been reviewed and approved based on the project requirements, user needs, and system objectives. The proposed design focuses on usability, scalability, security, and efficient medical report analysis using Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Design Objectives

* Provide a user-friendly interface for uploading medical reports.
* Ensure accurate extraction of medical information using OCR.
* Implement AI-based analysis for identifying abnormal values.
* Generate easy-to-understand health summaries.
* Maintain secure storage and management of medical data.
* Support future enhancements and scalability.
